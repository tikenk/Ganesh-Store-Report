# Ganesh-Store-Report
Ganesh Store Data Analysis

Introduction:
A Store called Ganesh Store sells goods through multiple online channels.
Objective:
Create an annual report for the year 2022 to better understand the customers and grow sales in 2023.
Ganesh Store Annual Insights:
1.	Most sales in March.
2.	Women customers are higher in number than men.
3.	92% of the packages are delivered successfully but 3% of orders are cancelled, 3% are returned, and 2% are refunded.
4.	Maharashtra, Karnataka and Uttar Pradesh are the top 3 states.
5.	Amazon, Flipkart and Myntra channels are the max contributors.
Conclusion to improve Ganesh Store sales:
Target women customers of the age group (30-49 yrs) living in Maharashtra, Karnataka and Uttar Pradesh by showing ads/offers/coupons available on Amazon, Flipkart and Myntra.
